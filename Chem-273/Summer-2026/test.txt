    <h4 style="margin-top: 30px;"><strong>Course Format</strong></h4>
    <p>This is an asynchronous online course consisting of 3 hours of lectures per week; 1 hour of discussion per week; and 1 hour of lab per week to complete this course in 13 weeks.</p>
    <p>&nbsp;</p>
    <h4 style="margin-top: 30px;"><strong>Course Requirements</strong></h4>
    <p>Each student is required to view all of the online lectures, do all the lecture exercises, submit all homework assignments, and attend discussion and lab sessions. A laptop, workstation, or access to a UNIX-style account is required.</p>
